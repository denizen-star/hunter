Job Description

We are seeking a Manager, Analytics Engineering to lead our analytics team and drive data-driven decision making across the organization. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in building scalable data pipelines and leading cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and mentor a team of analytics engineers
  • Design and implement scalable data pipelines using modern tools
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business requirements
  • Ensure data quality and reliability across all analytics products
  • Drive best practices in data engineering and analytics

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in analytics engineering or data engineering
  • Strong proficiency in SQL and Python
  • Experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
  • Experience with dbt or similar transformation tools
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Experience with orchestration tools (Airflow, Prefect, etc.)
