Reports Activities

2) Know What’s Working

Track New, Status Changes, Active, Rejected, and total Actions. View Daily Activities with collapsible sections and accurate period filters (Yesterday/7/30/All).

Research Real‑time Credible

3) Research in Real Time

Hunter performs live web searches for each application — news, personnel, products/services, and competitors — then structures results with links and graceful N/A handling. Company‑specific fallbacks avoid dead‑ends.

Matching Tech Scoring

4) Match Intelligently

Hunter evaluates features and technologies with weighted scoring, skill equivalence, and overqualification bonuses. The Technologies section shows ✅ matched, ⚠ partial, and ❌ missing for quick gap checks.

Documents Templates Intros

5) Tailor Materials in Minutes

Create longer, copy‑ready intro messages (≈275/500/500) and ML‑enhanced cover letters that reflect research findings. Use Templates and the TemplateManager service to reuse best‑performing structures.

Cleanup Focus

6) Keep It Clean

When a job is rejected, Hunter cleans up generated artifacts, regenerates a minimal summary, updates the dashboard link, and redirects you back to the pipeline.

How to Start

Quick Start

Install, run, and create your first application in minutes. Use the dashboard to monitor progress, research each company, tailor your materials, and iterate with reports and activities.
