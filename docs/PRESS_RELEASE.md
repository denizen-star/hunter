## For Immediate Release

### Hunter Launches GTM Release: An End‑to‑End Platform for Faster, Smarter Job Applications

October 30, 2025 — Hunter announced general availability of its go‑to‑market release: a modern, AI‑assisted platform that helps candidates research companies, tailor application materials, and track progress from first click to offer. This release packages a robust dashboard, real‑time research, intelligent matching, and reusable templates into one seamless workflow.

### Key Benefits

- Operate your pipeline: Tabbed dashboard, “Active” view, and advanced sorting provide instant clarity on priorities.
- Tailor materials in minutes: Longer intro messages, ML‑enhanced cover letters, and reusable templates accelerate personalization.
- Research with confidence: Real‑time company insights (news, personnel, products, competitors) with structured, credible output.
- Match intelligently: Feature‑weighted scoring, skill equivalence, and overqualification recognition increase signal over noise.
- Prove impact: Reports, Daily Activities, and period‑accurate analytics reveal what’s working across your job search.

### Who It’s For

- Individual contributors and managers running high‑volume searches
- Career switchers who need clarity on gaps and evidence
- Senior candidates who want to highlight overqualification without underselling

### What’s New in This Release

### Product Enhancements

#### Dashboard & Navigation
- Tabbed dashboard with nine status tabs and dynamic counts
- Advanced sorting (Updated, Applied, Posted Date, Match %) per tab
- Posted Date extraction and display in application cards
- Dashboard set as default route; new “Active” tab to focus on in-flight applications
- Daily Activities page with collapsible day sections and activity counts
- Soft Nordic design system across the app with full‑width layout, spacing, and UI refinements
- Templates page with collapsible cards and updated navigation for quick access

#### Matching & Qualifications
- Enhanced qualification analysis with feature counts and weighted scoring
- Technology matcher to reduce hallucinations; resume technology caching
- Overqualification recognition with tiered bonus scoring
- Improved parsing, normalization, and skill equivalence mappings
- Technologies section with clear status indicators (✅ matched / ⚠ partial / ❌ missing)

#### Research System
- Real‑time web search for company research (news, personnel, products, competitors)
- Company‑specific fallbacks for major brands with authentic mission/vision statements
- Structured Research tab output with links and graceful N/A handling

#### Reports & Analytics
- Reports page with charts and pipeline analytics
- Metrics including New, Status Changes, Active, Rejected, and Actions
- Correct period logic (Yesterday, 7 Days, 30 Days, All Time) with consistent timezone handling

#### Documents & Content Generation
- Longer intro messages (≈275/500/500 chars) as copy‑ready boxes
- Cover letter template enhanced with ML findings and improved structure
- Templates system and TemplateManager service/APIs for reusable content

#### Resume & Tech Extraction
- Resume technology extraction and caching pipeline for faster, reliable matching
- Expanded database technology coverage and removal of Scala to avoid false positives

#### Summary Page Improvements
- Skills tab with individual pills and accurate counts
- Raw Entry tab shows original job description input
- Hiring Team section restored with proper empty‑state handling
- Additional Insights section with conditional display

#### Workflow & Cleanup
- Rejection cleanup flow: removes unneeded artifacts, regenerates minimal summary, and auto‑redirects to dashboard

#### Documentation
- Comprehensive documentation reorganization into `docs/` with index, maintenance guide, and detailed release notes
