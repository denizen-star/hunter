Networking Rewards

The Networking Rewards System gamifies your networking efforts by awarding badges and points based on your progress with each contact. As you advance contacts through different relationship stages, you earn badges that reflect your networking achievements. Points are cumulative and help you track your overall networking success.

How It Works

Points are cumulative: When a contact reaches a specific status, you earn points for all badges up to and including that status. For example, if a contact reaches "In Conversation", you earn: Deep Diver (+10) + Profile Magnet (+3) + Qualified Lead (+15) + Conversation Starter (+20) = 48 total points.

Badge Display

Badges appear in two locations:

  • Networking Contact Profile: In the header section, showing all 8 badges with earned badges displaying the "W" (won) version and unearned badges showing the "U" (unearned) version.
  • Application Hero Section: Shows the highest badge achieved and the next badge to earn for contacts linked to that application.

Badge States

  • Earned (W): Displayed when a contact has reached the required status for that badge. Shows the full-color "W" version of the badge image.
  • Unearned (U): Displayed when a contact has not yet reached the required status. Shows the grayscale "U" version of the badge image.

Recurring Badges

Some badges can be earned multiple times. The Relationship Manager badge awards points each time you perform a check-in with a contact who has reached "Strong Connection" status.
